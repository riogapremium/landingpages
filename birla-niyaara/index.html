About Birla Niyaara
Tower Silas at Birla Niyaara, Worli, epitomizes opulence with its 4, 5, and 6 BHK residences and duplexes. Crafted for connoisseurs of luxury, this architectural masterpiece offers awe-inspiring interiors and panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and Mumbai's skyline. With over 30,000 sq. ft. of exclusive amenities, including sky terraces, infinity pools, and wellness zones, it blends contemporary living with timeless elegance, setting a new benchmark for luxury in South Mumbai.
