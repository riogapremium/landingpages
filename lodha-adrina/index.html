About Lodha Adrina
Adrina - The final tower at Lodha The Park in Worli. When you live at Lodha Adrina, 'luxury' is not merely a word- it's a lifestyle. If you're craving the beauty of nature, walk out into a private forest, stroll beautifully designed gardens, or make the most of sporting grounds.
