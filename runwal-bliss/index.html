About Runwal Bliss
Welcome to Runwal Bliss, a premium residential township project at Kanjurmarg East, built around a 150+ years old Banyan tree. It has been designed to see more... offer its residents a tranquil and peaceful haven amidst the hustle bustle of the city, without missing out on any of the city’s conveniences. It is a part of the Runwal City Centre township, built on the live, learn, work, play theme. This project is a part of Runwal City Centre, a 36 Acre township.
